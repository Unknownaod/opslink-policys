OpsLinkCAD – Cookie Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how OpsLinkCAD (“the Service”), owned and operated by OpsLink Systems, uses cookies and similar technologies. By accessing or using the Service, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this Policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when visiting a website or using a web application. Cookies allow us to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Remember your preferences
  • Improve performance
  • Secure user sessions

Cookies do not give us access to your device, files, or personal data beyond what you explicitly provide.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are required for the core functionality of OpsLinkCAD. Without them, the Service cannot operate correctly.

  • Authentication cookies
  • Session management cookies
  • Security verification cookies

2.2 Preference Cookies

Used to remember settings such as:

  • Theme (dark/light)
  • Language preferences
  • User interface settings

2.3 Performance & Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how users interact with OpsLinkCAD.

We may use analytics strictly to:

  • Diagnose performance issues
  • Monitor service stability
  • Improve reliability

We do not use advertising cookies or targeted marketing cookies.

2.4 Third-Party Cookies

The only third-party cookies come from:

  • Stripe – used for checkout, fraud detection, and subscription management

OpsLinkCAD does not use any other external cookie providers.

3. How We Use Cookies

OpsLinkCAD uses cookies to:

  • Keep sessions active after login
  • Maintain user account states
  • Secure account access
  • Enable billing features and payments
  • Enhance platform performance

4. Managing Cookies

You may block or delete cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling essential cookies may result in:

  • Inability to log in
  • Dashboard not loading
  • Broken sections of the application
  • Checkout/payment issues

OpsLinkCAD cannot function properly without required cookies.

5. Data Collected by Cookies

Cookies may collect:

  • Session identifiers
  • Authentication tokens
  • Theme preferences
  • Language settings
  • Basic usage data (non-identifiable)

Cookies do not store:

  • Passwords
  • Raw personal data
  • Billing information
  • Sensitive information

6. Security & Cookie Integrity

OpsLink Systems protects cookies using:

  • Secure HTTPS-only cookie transmission
  • Session expiration mechanisms
  • Modern cryptographic standards

We do not allow:

  • Third-party advertising cookies
  • Tracking cookies unrelated to OpsLinkCAD
