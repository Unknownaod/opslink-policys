OpsLinkCAD – Data Processing Addendum (DPA)

Effective Date: November 19th, 2025

Last Updated: November 19th, 2025

This Data Processing Addendum (“DPA”) forms part of the OpsLinkCAD Terms of Service and governs the processing of personal data by OpsLinkCAD (“the Service”), a system owned and operated by OpsLink Systems.

This DPA is designed to comply with global privacy regulations, including:

  • EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • UK GDPR
  • Canadian PIPEDA
  • US State Privacy Laws (CCPA/CPRA, etc.)
  • Any applicable international data protection standards

1. Definitions

  • “Controller” – You, the user or organization using OpsLinkCAD.
  • “Processor” – OpsLink Systems, operating OpsLinkCAD.
  • “Data Subject” – Any individual whose personal data is processed.
  • “Personal Data” – Any information relating to an identifiable person.
  • “Processing” – Any operation performed on personal data.

2. Roles & Responsibilities

2.1 The Controller

You determine:

  • What data is entered into OpsLinkCAD
  • How long your data is retained
  • What information is collected from members of your community

2.2 The Processor (OpsLink Systems)

We process data solely to provide OpsLinkCAD services.

We do not:

  • Sell data
  • Share data for advertising
  • Process data for unrelated purposes

3. Data We Process On Your Behalf

When you use OpsLinkCAD, you may process personal data belonging to your members, such as:

  • Names / identifiers
  • Unit IDs, departments, call data
  • Community/staff records
  • Operational CAD/MDT data
  • Notes and logs you enter

OpsLink Systems processes this data strictly under your instruction.

4. Security Measures (GDPR Article 32)

OpsLink Systems implements appropriate technical and organizational security measures, including:

  • Encrypted passwords
  • Secure servers and hardened infrastructure
  • Access controls
  • Least-permission internal access policies
  • Regular monitoring for security breaches
  • Data isolation between communities

5. Subprocessors

OpsLink Systems uses the following subprocessors:

  • Stripe – Billing and subscription management
  • Hosting provider(s) – Cloud infrastructure for running OpsLinkCAD

All subprocessors are bound by the same data protection obligations.

6. International Transfers

Your data may be processed or stored in countries outside your jurisdiction. By using OpsLinkCAD, you consent to these transfers.

7. Data Breach Notification (GDPR Article 33)

If OpsLink Systems becomes aware of a confirmed personal data breach, we will notify all affected Controllers as soon as reasonably possible.

8. Data Subject Rights

OpsLink Systems will assist you in fulfilling requests from your members, including:

  • Access
  • Correction
  • Deletion
  • Export/Portability

You are responsible for responding to Data Subject requests.

9. Data Retention & Deletion

OpsLink Systems retains your operational data only for as long as your account or community exists.

Upon deletion of an account, OpsLinkCAD will:

  • Erase operational data
  • Remove personal identifiable information
  • Retain necessary logs required by law (if applicable)

10. Audit Rights

OpsLink Systems may provide documentation necessary to demonstrate compliance with this DPA.

12. Amendments

OpsLink Systems may modify this DPA at any time. Updated versions are effective immediately upon posting.
